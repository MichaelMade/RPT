# RPT Support

Need help with RPT, found a bug, or have a feature suggestion? Email [moorem88@gmail.com](mailto:moorem88@gmail.com?subject=RPT%20Support).

To help diagnose an issue, include:

- The RPT app version shown in **Settings > About**
- Your iPhone or iPad model and iOS/iPadOS version
- What you expected to happen and the steps that produced the issue
- A screenshot or screen recording, if useful, with any personal workout information removed

## Purchases

If an RPT Pro purchase is not recognized, open the RPT Pro screen and choose **Restore Purchases** while signed in with the Apple Account used for the purchase. Apple handles all billing and purchase records; RPT does not receive payment-card details.

## Data and privacy

RPT stores workout data on your device and does not provide an account-based cloud backup. Deleting the app can also delete its local data. Review the [RPT Privacy Policy](https://github.com/MichaelMade/RPT/blob/master/Privacy%20Policy) for details.
